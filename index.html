About Us

Shiv Construction is a reliable construction service provider with years of experience in residential buildings, commercial projects, wooden houses, cottages, mud houses, and renovation works. We provide customized construction solutions that blend traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering.

Our team includes experienced engineers, site supervisors, and skilled craftsmen who specialize in natural, eco-friendly, and sustainable construction techniques. Whether it’s a modern concrete home, a wooden cottage, or a traditional mud house, we ensure durability, comfort, and aesthetic appeal.


Our Vision:
